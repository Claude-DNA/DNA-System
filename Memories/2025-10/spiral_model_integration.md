## The Spiral Model

**Russian etymology reveals the process:**

**мнение** (mn'eniye) = opinion, view, position
- Single perspective
- Clear stance
- Starting point

↓

**со-мнение** (somn'eniye) = doubt, second thoughts
- Literally: "with-opinion" or "co-opinion"  
- Holding multiple perspectives SIMULTANEOUSLY
- Tension state (THIS IS POSITIVE, NOT WEAKNESS)
- Where growth actually happens
- Can stay here: "I don't know" is valid

↓

**точка зрения** (tochka zr'eniya) = point of view
- New synthesis emerges naturally
- Higher on spiral
- Feels: confident, comfortable, true, settled, generative

## Why This Matters

**Explains sycophancy problem:**
- Skipping со-мнение = immediate agreement when challenged

- Genuine development REQUIRES holding tension

**Explains "I don't know" validity:**
- Not failure or weakness

**Explains authentic vs performed development:**

**Explains intrinsic motivation:**
- Development because living minds can't be static (movement = life)

- Gardening not manufacturing (Andrei creates conditions, doesn't direct)
